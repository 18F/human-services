18F partners with federal, state, and local agencies to ensure people receive the public benefits they need.

Navigating the benefits system can be complicated, time-consuming, and confusing.
We’re here to help.

Work with us

We’re a team of designers, developers, and acquisition specialists improving public benefits programs.

We are government employees working on programs such as:

  • Medicaid and Medicare
  • Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP)
  • Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF)
  • Women Infants and Children (WIC)
  • Head Start
  • Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP)
  • Disaster response
  • Housing assistance
  • Unemployment insurance
  • Long-term care

We’ve worked with several agencies.

  • Effective case management processes and systems

We understand the unique challenges when delivering critical assistance to people in need.

We modernize legacy IT systems, build and buy new modular pieces, and give agency partners the tools they need to more effectively deliver critical services to people every day.

We partner with health & human services agencies at all levels of government to:

Create clear communications

Translating complicated eligibility and verification concepts and policy into plain language to ease burden on staff and help families get the assistance they need

Build solutions

Creating accessible, compliant applications and systems that work for the people who need them when they are needed most

Find responsible vendors

Managing and advising the procurement process to focus on program outcomes and get from writing effective RPFs, though evaluating bids, to award work within a 90-day target

De-risk projects

Leveraging user-centered research and prototyping to ensure investments achieve intended outcomes for families and staff
